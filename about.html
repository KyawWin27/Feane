Our Story

We started off as a food truck in NYC, back in 2023, with just one simple goal: to serve fresh and great tasting food with hospitality. We began with just a dream, and a killer burger recipe has grown into a full sized restaurant loved by locals.

Our signature dish, the Classic Cheeseburger, is still made the same way it was on day one, with fresh ingredients, and lots of love.

But what really sets us apart? Our hard work. At Feane, we believe food should feel like home. Our team is small, but passionate, and genuinely loves what we do. Whether you're here for a quick lunch or a casual dinner with Feane, we always welcome you with hope and warmth.
