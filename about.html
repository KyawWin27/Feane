Our Story

Feane started as a small food truck back in 2015 with one simple goal: to serve great-tasting food that brings people together. What began with just a grill, a dream, and a killer burger recipe has grown into a full-sized restaurant loved by locals.

Our signature dish, the Classic Cheeseburger, is still made the same way it was on day one — with fresh ingredients, a secret sauce, and lots of love. Over time, we’ve added more to the menu: crispy fries, spicy chicken sandwiches, and creamy milkshakes, all crafted to comfort and satisfy.

But what really sets us apart? Our heart. At Feane, we believe food should feel like home. Our team is small, passionate, and genuinely loves what we do. Whether you're here for a quick lunch or a casual dinner with friends, we hope you feel the warmth of a place that cares.
